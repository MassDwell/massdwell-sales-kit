# MassDwell Market Intelligence Repository

## Purpose
Centralized archive of industry news, articles, and market validation data for:
- Business development insights
- Investor meeting preparation  
- Competitive intelligence
- Market trend analysis

## Structure

### `/adu-news/`
- ADU legislation and policy updates
- State/local regulatory changes
- Market growth data and trends
- Competitive landscape developments

### `/ai-construction/` 
- AI adoption in construction industry
- Technology trends and case studies
- Productivity and efficiency research
- Construction tech innovation

### `/industry-trends/`
- General construction market news
- Housing shortage and demand data
- Manufacturing and prefab developments
- Economic indicators affecting construction

### `/market-validation/`
- Market size estimates and TAM data
- Customer demand indicators
- Pricing and competitive analysis
- Success stories and case studies

## Article Format
Each article saved as `.md` file with standardized header:
```markdown
# [Article Title]
**Source:** [Publication]  
**Date:** [YYYY-MM-DD]  
**URL:** [Link]  
**Tags:** [category, relevance, priority]  
**Relevance to MassDwell:** [Brief summary]

## Summary
[Key points summary]

## Full Content
[Article content]
```

## Daily Integration
- Market intel scans add to repository
- Weekly summaries generated for review
- Monthly investor briefing compilation
- Quarterly market validation updates
